About the Quiz Game

Test your knowledge across multiple categories!

Game Overview

Welcome to our Quiz Game! Whether you're a trivia master or just want to have some fun, this game is designed to challenge you. Choose from multiple categories and levels, and put your knowledge to the test with 10 questions. Each question has a 15-second timer to keep the challenge exciting!

After completing all questions, you'll receive your score and be able to review all previous questions with their correct answers. It's a fun and educational way to test your knowledge, learn new facts, and improve your memory!

Game Features

  • ✔ Multiple categories to choose from: General Knowledge, Sports, Movies, Science, History, and more!
  • ✔ Timed quizzes: Each question has 15 seconds to solve, adding an extra challenge.
  • ✔ 10 questions per quiz: A set of 10 random questions based on your selected category and level.
  • ✔ Review your results: After completing the quiz, view your score and all questions with the correct answers.
  • ✔ Easy-to-use interface: Simple and interactive design with clear instructions and feedback.
